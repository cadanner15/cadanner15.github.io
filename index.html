Trine University Marketing and Communication Office Intern

From August to December of 2015, I interned in the Marketing and Communication office at Trine University. There, I learned Associated Press writing style, developed press releases promoting campus events, and wrote articles for the school website, some of which can be accessed here. Through this internship, I was able to hone my interviewing, photography, and news reporting skills.

Kohl's

I've been employed at Kohl's as a customer service associate from November of 2015 to the present. Some of my tasks include addressing customer complaints and concerns, processing applications for Kohl's credit cards, ticketing and clearancing merchandise for purchase, and operating the register to complete sales, returns, exchanges, and post-sale adjustments. In June of 2016, I earned the silver pin that signifies that five different customers in one day mentioned my name within the context of a "highly satisfied" survey. I believe my employment at Kohl's has increased my multitasking and problem-solving skills tenfold!

Budget Inks

I was employed at Budget Inks, a small, industrial printer cartridge vendor in Angola, IN, from August of 2016 to May of 2017. I served as a customer retention specialist, and my responsibilities included selling, packing, and shipping ink to clients, as well as speaking with clients on the phone about their level of satisfaction with products.
